Frequently Asked Questions

Most recharges are processed instantly. However, during peak times or due to technical issues, it might take up to 15 minutes. If your recharge is not processed within 30 minutes, please contact our customer support.

If your recharge fails but the amount is deducted from your account, don't worry. The amount will be automatically refunded to your payment source within 5-7 working days. If you don't receive a refund within this timeframe, please contact our customer support with your transaction details.

Yes! You can recharge for any mobile number regardless of whether it belongs to you or someone else. Simply enter the mobile number you want to recharge for in the designated field during the recharge process.

You can check your current plan details by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Plan" section. Alternatively, most service providers allow you to check your plan details by dialing a USSD code like *121# or similar (check your provider's specific code).

When your plan expires, you'll be charged according to the base tariff plan. It's recommended to recharge with a new plan before your current one expires to avoid higher charges. If you've enabled Auto-Recharge, your plan will be automatically renewed upon expiry.

Yes, you can change your plan before it expires, but the remaining benefits of your current plan may be forfeited when you switch. Some providers offer plan change options that allow you to keep certain benefits. Check with your specific provider for their policy on plan changes.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.), mobile wallets, and more. You can save your payment methods for faster checkout in the future.

Yes, we use industry-standard security protocols to ensure your payment details are safe. Your card information is encrypted and we comply with PCI DSS standards. We never store complete card details on our servers.

Auto-Recharge automatically renews your plan when it expires. You can set up Auto-Recharge in your account settings by selecting a payment method and the plan you want to automatically renew. You'll receive notifications before each automatic recharge, and you can cancel this feature at any time.

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered mobile number or email address, and we'll send you a verification code. Use this code to create a new password. For security reasons, make sure your new password is strong and unique.

Yes, you can manage multiple mobile numbers from a single account. Go to "My Account" and select "Manage Numbers" to add additional mobile numbers. You can recharge any of these numbers, view their history, and manage their plans individually.

You can view your recharge history by logging into your account and navigating to "Transaction History" or "Recharge History" in the account section. Here, you'll find details of all your past recharges including date, amount, mobile number, and transaction status.
